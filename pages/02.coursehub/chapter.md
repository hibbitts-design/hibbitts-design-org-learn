> [!TIP]
> **New: Grav Helios Course Hub** – The successor to the Open Course Hub and Open MultiCourse Hub, now unified into a single installation for one or more courses. Built on the Grav Premium Helios theme for a significantly more refined visual experience, with automatic single or multi-course setup, context-aware search, visual course cards with images and badges, and rich content embedding (H5P, iFrames, Google Slides) – no custom design or coding required. [Grav Helios Course Hub on GitHub](https://github.com/hibbitts-design/grav-skeleton-helios-course-hub).
